REASON FOR CREATION OF ASILIA MBOLEA?

This was due to unreliable supply and poor quality of organic ferltilisers in most of tanzanian regions thus causing the smallholder farmers to use high costs in production meanwhile affecting their soil health, so a group of skilled and self initiated expertise created an idea of "ASILIA MBOLEA" which would then be a breakthrough to all challenges facing smallholder or even large scale farmers,to make more produce while, not affecting their soil
